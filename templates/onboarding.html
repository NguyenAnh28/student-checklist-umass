University of Massachusetts Amherst

Student Information

Checklist

  • Complete any necessary placement tests or assessments
  • Submit final transcripts and necessary documentation
  • Enroll in university health insurance
  • Register housing appointment
  • Attend mandatory first-year orientation
  • Meet with an academic advisor to register for classes
  • Obtain your UMass student ID card
  • Set up your university email account
  • Review and understand academic policies and resources
  • Familiarize yourself with campus facilities and services
